[Ollie]

[Disclaimer: I skate in regular stance, which means I skate with my left foot forward. If that doesn’t feel comfortable that means you are most likely goofy-footed. For those people, just mirror what I describe. So if I say my left foot, think of your right foot instead. Step 1: Position your body so that your shoulders are in line with your skateboard. Step 2: Put your left foot just behind or on top of the four bolts that anchor your trucks onto your skateboard. Step 3: Put your right foot on the tail of the skateboard. In other words, put it on the back most raised portion of the skateboard. Step 4: While jumping push down the tail of the board with your right foot so that it hits the ground. Step 4½: As soon as you complete step 4 begin sliding your left foot up the board until you reach the nose. After this prepare to land. Step 5: Land and roll away! You Successfully Ollied! ]

[Kickflip]

[Disclaimer: I skate in regular stance, which means I skate with my left foot forward. If that doesn’t feel comfortable that means you are most likely goofy-footed. For those people, just mirror what I describe. So if I say my left foot, think of your right foot instead. Step 1: Position your body so that your shoulders are in line with your skateboard. Step 2: Put your left foot just behind the four bolts that anchor your trucks onto your skateboard. Step 3: Put your right foot on the tail of the skateboard. In other words, put it on the back most raised portion of the skateboard. Step 4: While jumping push down the tail of the board with your right foot so that it hits the ground. Step 4½: As soon as you complete step 4 flick your left foot off the heel side of the board. Step 5: Wait for the board to flip a full 360 degrees then catch the board with your right foot and prepare to land. Step 6: Land and roll away! You are so cool! You can Kickflip now! ]
