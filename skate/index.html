Welcome all who are learning how to skateboard! Have you ever wanted to learn to skateboard but had no teacher? Thanks to modern technology I can assist in your Goal to skateboard well! On this website, our goal is to teach you how to ollie and kickflip! I’m sure you’ve seen skateboarders in your neighborhood or around your town/ city. With a bit of practice, you will be able to shred anywhere you go. As long as there is smooth, solid ground! On this home page, you will find links to the Ollie and Kickflip sub-pages, as well as a link to my Instagram. On Instagram, I often post skateboarding videos! Feel free to follow me on Instagram to stay up to date with the latest things going on in my life!

I’m sure you are wondering how expensive this service is. Believe it or not, this is a free service. It is hosted here on the World Wide Web for anyone to access! You can support me by following my Instagram and staying up to date with me there!

Extra Tips

While learning how to skate I suggest either going to a skatepark or finding a place that has smooth ground. That ground will become your friend very quickly. Expect to fall often, It’s just something that comes with being a skater! Embrace it!
